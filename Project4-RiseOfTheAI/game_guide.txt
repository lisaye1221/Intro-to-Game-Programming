Capture the bunnies before the time runs out

Level 1: 

This bunny will start to run away if you get too close to it.
Chase it!

Level 2: 

The bunny will copy your movement and seem to move as fast as you.
But it will only do so if you're on the second level...

Level 3: 

These two bunnies will jump up to the 4th level when they see you.
You can't reach the 4th level though. 
They'll come back down after you leave the third level.
